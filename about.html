About This Project

This project is a learning and interaction platform designed for students and learners to collaborate, share knowledge, and grow together.

It allows users to:

Create groups and engage in Q&A sessions.

Earn ranks and badges based on contributions.

Connect with mentors and seniors for guidance.

The platform provides AI-powered learning recommendations to help users stay on track.

It also includes an event calendar to keep students updated on seminars, workshops, and college activities.

The goal is to create a supportive community where students can learn, grow, and succeed together.
